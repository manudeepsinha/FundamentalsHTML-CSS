Hello there! I'm going to share with you my quick, easy, and tasty version of nighttime Maggie which helps me get through the night. Follow the instructions and try it!
Ingredients you're gonna need are:

  • Maggie
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Mustard seeds

Now below is the recipe:

  1. Pour 2 tablespoon of oil in the pan and let it heat
  2. Put 1/2 tablespoon of Mustard seeds in hot oil
  3. Pour 2 cups of water after seeds are popped
  4. Bring the water to boil and then add Maggie masala
  5. Stir well so no lumps are formed
  6. Put the Maggie cake inside the pan and let it cook for about 5-7 mins until the water is dried up
  7. Serve hot and enjoy delicious Maggie!
